Description

Capture, view, and share the memories you create on a large 16.71 cm (6.58) display with the Vivo Y53s. It features a 64 MP super high-definition rear camera that captures all the possible details and colours. The smartphone runs on a minimalist Funtouch OS 11.1 (based on Android 11) and offers you a smooth user experience.

Product Description

Large Screen Display

The Vivo Y53s smartphone features a large 16.71 cm (6.58) screen and displays visuals in FHD+ (2408x1080) and P3 colour gamut. This helps provide you with an immersive experience, especially while watching videos or playing games. In addition to this, the display also helps filter out harmful blue light to reduce the strain caused on the eyes.

Integrated Fingerprint Scanner

The smartphone's Side Fingerprint design combines the fingerprint scanner and the power button, so you can not only turn on the display but also unlock your phone. The fingerprint scanner is quick and responsive, taking up to 0.24 seconds to unlock from a lit screen.

64 MP Rear Camera

The Vivo Y53s helps you capture images with stunning details, thanks to its 64 MP primary rear camera. The 64 million pixels in each image help you see the tiniest of details, from a single hair to even eyelashes.

Expandable RAM

To make multitasking and other processor-heavy operations easier, the Y53s allows you to allocate up to 3 GB of ROM towards the existing 8 GB RAM of this smartphone.

Large ROM

The Y53s boasts a large storage capacity of up to 128 GB, with an additional three-card slot that lets you expand the memory up to 1 TB. Now you can store all your favourite movies and shows on your phone to access them whenever you want.

Eye Auto-focus

The Y53s makes use of PDAF technology to ensure that the focus of the rear camera is locked on the eyes, making it easier to track and capture images of moving objects. Don’t miss out on any fleeting moments, just strike a pose, and let the camera do the rest.
